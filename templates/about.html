About the LiteFeet Ledger

The LiteFeet Ledger is a living digital ledger preserving the history, people, battles, events, awards, and evolution of LiteFeet culture — powered by community submissions, verified event data, transparent community review, and an AI ledger assistant.

What the ledger will hold

The ledger will collect dancer profiles, event records, battle results, source links, LiteFeet Awards history, Hall of Fame records, movement history, media links, and community-submitted corrections.

How verification works

Some records will be verified by hosts, dancers, judges, video sources, or ledger reviewers. Other records may need community review. When something needs verification, people will be able to mark it true, false, or debatable and submit a source link for review.

This ledger is still being built

If you have a source link, correction, event result, flyer, video, or context that belongs here, submit it for review and help complete the record.
