About the LiteFeet Archive

The LiteFeet Archive is a living digital archive preserving the history, people, battles, events, awards, and evolution of LiteFeet culture — powered by community submissions, verified event data, transparent community review, and an AI archive assistant.

What the archive will hold

The archive will collect dancer profiles, event records, battle results, source links, LiteFeet Awards history, Hall of Fame records, movement history, media links, and community-submitted corrections.

How verification works

Some records will be verified by hosts, dancers, judges, video sources, or archive reviewers. Other records may need community review. When something needs verification, people will be able to mark it true, false, or debatable and submit a source link for review.

This archive is still being built

If you have a source link, correction, event result, flyer, video, or context that belongs here, submit it for review and help complete the record.
